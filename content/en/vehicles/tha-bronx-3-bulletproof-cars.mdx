### Honda
The Honda serves as the entry-level bulletproof option for players with limited funds. It is widely considered a slow vehicle, earning it the nickname "the snail" among experienced drivers. While its top speed is capped around 167 mph, its handling is relatively stable. It is not recommended for high-speed chases, but it offers basic protection for players starting their journey.

### Dodge Ram TRX
Priced at $74,000, the TRX is a massive, heavy-duty truck that commands respect on the road. It is highly favored for its imposing presence and fully protected windows. On straightaways, the TRX can reach an astonishing top speed of nearly 400 mph. However, its heavy chassis results in sluggish initial acceleration, and its braking power is notoriously weak. Drivers must begin braking well in advance to avoid losing control.

### Ford Bronco
The Bronco is a sturdy, large-bodied SUV that offers excellent defensive coverage. It features smooth handling and highly reliable brakes, easily outperforming the TRX in stopping distance tests. It is a solid, middle-of-the-road choice for players who prioritize stability and control over raw top-end speed.

### Infinity Q60
Retailing at $73,000, the Q60 is a premier getaway vehicle. While its top speed is limited to 172 mph, its acceleration is phenomenal. It can reach its maximum velocity in roughly 20 seconds, making it incredibly effective for escaping tight alleys and losing pursuers. It also features a sleek body design and an integrated rear spoiler.

<Callout type="success" title="Acceleration Advantage">

### Dodge 2005 RT Magnum
At just $45,000, the RT Magnum is one of the best value-for-money purchases in the game. It drives similarly to a classic Hellcat, pushing speeds up to 400 mph. It features a distinct engine roar and offers incredible straight-line performance that rivals vehicles costing twice as much.

### Infinity Q50S
The Q50S is a revamped sports sedan costing approximately $50,000. It reaches a top speed of 288 mph and features a unique engine sound that resembles a futuristic spaceship. Its balanced handling and responsive brakes make it a highly reliable choice for daily driving and tactical operations alike.

### Bentley
Despite its high price tag, the Bentley is widely considered a poor investment for active combat. It suffers from sluggish acceleration, heavy turning, and a bulky frame. While it provides excellent passenger safety, its lack of agility makes it an easy target for coordinated attacks.

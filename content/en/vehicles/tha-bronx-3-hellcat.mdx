The **HellCat XD Beam** is a premium weapon accessory gamepass priced at **400 Robux**. When purchased, it equips your compatible firearms with a high-visibility neon laser sight. This laser beam greatly improves your hip-fire tracking, allowing you to maintain accurate crosshair placement on moving targets without needing to aim down sights. It is highly favored by aggressive PvP players who specialize in close-quarters combat and \"corner-peeking\" tactics.

### Hellcat Flash Drum Overview

The **Hellcat Flash Drum** is a legendary high-capacity magazine gamepass that has historically gone on and off-sale in the Roblox Store. This modification replaces standard magazines with a massive drum, allowing players to fire extended salvos without reloading. In a game where reloading at the wrong moment means a trip to the Medical Center, the Flash Drum provides unmatched sustained fire capabilities.

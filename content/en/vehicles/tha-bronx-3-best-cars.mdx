### Detailed Performance Breakdown

#### McLaren ($375,000)
The McLaren is the ultimate speed demon in the game. Reaching an incredible top speed of 210 mph, it allows you to cross the entire map in seconds. It features a fully detailed interior and exceptional straight-line acceleration. However, its high speed makes cornering difficult for inexperienced drivers.

#### Lamborghini Urus ($300,000 / Lambo Gamepass)
Often referred to as the Lambo SUV, this is the go-to vehicle for serious grinders. It is quiet, boasts excellent handling, and has a functional trunk for moving loot. Because it seats four players, it is the perfect choice for running organized bank robberies with your crew. You can also purchase this vehicle directly from the Roblox Store via the Lambo gamepass for 800 Robux.

#### Mercedes-Benz GLE 53 ($120,000)
A fan-favorite in the Benz lineup, the GLE 53 combines luxury with raw power. Reaching speeds of up to 206 mph, this massive SUV handles surprisingly well for its size. It is a highly reliable vehicle for driving through hostile territory.

#### Maserati Ghibli ($100,000)
The Ghibli is widely considered the best "drilling" car in the game. It features crisp, predictable handling, making it incredibly easy to slide around tight street corners during drive-bys. While its top speed is capped at a modest 167 mph, its agility and customization options (such as carbon hoods and tinted lights) make it a must-own.

Purchasing your vehicle is only the first step. To get the most out of your ride, you need to understand how to spawn, customize, and safely utilize your trunk storage.

### Spawning Your Vehicle

To get your car on the road, you must visit the designated Car Dealership located in the Northwest section of the main Bronx region.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Visit the Dealership">
    Travel to the Northwest region of the main map and enter the Car Dealership building. This area is a designated Safe Zone.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Interact with the Spawner">
    Walk up to the vehicle spawner terminal inside the dealership lobby to open your garage menu.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Select and Spawn">
    Choose your desired vehicle from your owned list and click spawn. Your vehicle will appear in the designated parking spots outside.
  </Step>
</Steps>

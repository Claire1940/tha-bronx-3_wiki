<Section icon={Target} title="A-Tier and B-Tier: Switches and Drums">

The A-Tier is defined by "Switches" or "Buttons." These modifications turn standard handguns into full-auto powerhouses. A G21 with a button or a Cyan button pistol can often out-trade a rifle in a tight hallway. However, they are ranked slightly lower than Fullies because of their smaller frames and sometimes unpredictable spray patterns.

B-Tier weapons are characterized by their massive magazine capacity. Using an NSR or a VP9 with a 100-round drum allows you to keep firing while your opponent is forced to reload. This is a "spray and pray" meta that works exceptionally well for beginners who haven't mastered headshot precision yet.

<Section icon={Target} title="How to Rob ATMs: Tools and Methods">

Robbing ATMs is one of the fastest progression pillars in the game, but you cannot simply walk up and take the cash. You must utilize specialized tools. Players can choose between physical drilling, which takes time but yields high payouts, or card hacking, which is instantaneous but relies on successful swipe probabilities.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Acquire Your Robbery Tools">
    Visit the **Roof Shop** (located upstairs in the same building as the Soda Pop Store) to purchase a **Drill** or **C4**. Alternatively, visit the **Laundromat** to purchase **Swipe Cards** or **Deftun Cards** for hacking.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Equip Protective Gear">
    Equip a **Mask** to hide your identity and prepare a weapon from **Gunstop** to defend yourself against opportunistic players looking to steal your loot.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Execute the Heist">
    Approach any of the active **tha bronx 3 atm locations**. If drilling, interact with the machine and wait 1-2 minutes while defending the perimeter. If hacking, insert your swipe card and watch for the green acceptance light.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Secure and Clean the Cash">
    Collect the red dirty money piles. Immediately transport the cash to the **Laundromat** to clean it, then deposit it at the **Bank** to secure your hard-earned funds.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Police and Player Danger">
Drilling an ATM triggers loud noise and alerts nearby players. Always keep your vehicle running nearby with an open door for a rapid escape if the situation turns hostile.
</Callout>

### The Money Laundering Loop

Processing your cash requires moving through high-traffic areas. Follow this structured route to minimize your exposure:

| Step | Action | Location | Risk | Key Tip |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **1. Collect Cash** | Rob ATMs or complete routes | Various ATMs | High | Do not carry too much at once |
| **2. Count Money** | Place red money in counters | Laundromat | Low | Watch out for door campers |
| **3. Pack Bag** | Put counted cash into duffel | Laundromat | Medium | Bag must match your counter |
| **4. Deposit** | Take duffel bag to red line | Bank | High | Park vehicle close to entrance |

<Callout type="success" title="Clean Cash Secured">

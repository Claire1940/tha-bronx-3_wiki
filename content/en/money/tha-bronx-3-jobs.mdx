  <Step num="2" title="Load and Deliver">

These logistics roles are best suited for players who have already invested in **Extra Trunk Space**. Having more storage in your vehicle allows you to carry more supplies per trip, reducing the number of times you have to return to the warehouse and thereby decreasing your exposure to potential ambush points on the southernmost highway.

To successfully pull off the **tha bronx 3 new robbery**, you cannot simply walk in. The game mechanics require two specific items that must be purchased beforehand. <Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Robbery Walkthrough">

Executing the **tha bronx 3 new robbery** requires a specific sequence of actions. If you miss a step, such as forgetting to bring the bag, you will find yourself standing in a room full of loot with no way to carry it. Follow this progression to ensure a successful heist.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Acquire Tools">
    Visit the Hardware Store to purchase the Sledgehammer. Then, head to the Market area, go upstairs, and buy the Bank Bag from the specialized vendor. Ensure both are equipped in your hotbar.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Wait for Nightfall">
    Monitor the in-game time. The "Wireless and Electronics" store only becomes "robbable" once the sun sets. If you try to break the glass during the day, you will receive a notification stating the store is not robbable at this time.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Breach the Perimeter">
    Approach the front windows with your Sledgehammer drawn. Hit the glass panes approximately 5 times. The glass will shatter, allowing you to jump inside the showroom.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Loot the Phones">
    Walk up to the display cases and interact with the phones. Your Bank Bag UI will show the items being added (e.g., 0/5 to 5/5). Once the bag is full, you cannot pick up more items.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Cash Out">
    Exit the store and return to the Black Market NPC where you bought the bag. Interact with the NPC to sell the phones. You will receive approximately $3,250 for a full standard bag.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Efficiency Bonus">

</Callout>

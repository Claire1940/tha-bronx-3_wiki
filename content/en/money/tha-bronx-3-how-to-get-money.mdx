<Section icon={Swords} title="The Sledgehammer Grinding Route">

The Sledgehammer job is an exceptional mid-tier grinding method that bridges the gap between passive jobs and high-risk heists. To begin this method, you must locate the Exotic Shop, situated on the western street near the Gunstop block. Inside, you can purchase a Sledgehammer for a flat rate of $400. Once equipped, you will use the Location tool to scan the map for active target buildings, which are marked with bright red exclamation points.

Once you arrive at a targeted building, pull out your Sledgehammer and smash the brick wall to gain entry. Inside, you will find multiple piles of cash scattered across the floor. This cash is classified as Dirty Money, meaning you must transport it to the Dirty Money Laundromat near Gunstop to wash it using the money counters. The cleaning process takes roughly 30 seconds, after which the cash is entirely safe to deposit.

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="The Ice Fruit Cup Cooking Method">

For players looking for a consistent, high-yield farming loop, the Ice Fruit Cup and Kool-Aid cooking method is unmatched. This process requires a kitchen, making it highly recommended that you first purchase a permanent residence, such as the Penthouse near the Car Dealership, which costs $4,000 in-game cash. Once you have secured a home, head to the Exotic Shop or Zotic Shop in South Bronx to purchase your raw cooking ingredients.

To cook a perfect Ice Fruit Cup, you must buy an Ice Bag, Fiji Water, and Fresh Water. Bring these ingredients to your kitchen stove and interact with it to start the cooking process. You must wait for the cooking timer to complete fully; taking the product off the stove prematurely results in a smaller portion, which significantly reduces your payout. Once finished, collect your completed cup and drive to the Ice Fruit Truck located in the sky area near the southernmost highway.

<Section icon={Shield} title="High-Risk Heists & Robberies">

If you have secured a decent weapon and a fast getaway vehicle, high-risk robberies offer the fastest payouts in the game. The premier robbery location is **On the Radar**, which sits directly opposite the Laundromat. Walk through the blue door, smash the glass partition blocking the back office, and loot the three large piles of cash inside. This run yields a guaranteed $27,250 in dirty money, but it is heavily contested by rival players.

Alternatively, you can target ATM machines located at Dronald's or the western Gas Station. Smashing or shooting these machines drops heavy cash bags that you must carry to the bank. Store registers at local delis also offer quick cash payouts with slightly less risk than the main heists.

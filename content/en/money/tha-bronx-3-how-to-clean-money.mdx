<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="The Money Counter">
    Locate a Money Counter (found in specific hideouts and backrooms). Approach the machine and interact to place your red money inside. Wait for the progress bar to complete as it counts the bills.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Bagging the Cash">
    Once counted, the money will sit on the table. You must interact with it to "Put cash in bag." Continue this action until the loose bills are converted into a physical **Money Bag** that your character holds.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="The Bank Drop-Off">
    Travel to the Bank. Look for the large red interaction zone (often referred to as the "red thing"). While holding the bag, interact with this zone to deposit the funds. The money will instantly convert to black clean cash in your inventory.

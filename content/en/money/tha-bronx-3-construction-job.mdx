Finding the **tha bronx 3 construction job** requires a solid understanding of the city layout. To maximize your time, you should memorize the route between the construction site and the nearest Bank or Laundry. Carrying large amounts of cash from manual labor makes you a "walking ATM" for aggressive players.

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="How to Start Your First Shift">

Starting the **tha bronx 3 construction job** does not require a complex application process, but it does require following a specific sequence to ensure you are actually "on the clock" and earning rewards. 

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Gear Up and Prepare">
    Visit the Zotic Shop or a Deli to stock up on food. Working manual labor drains your character's stats faster than standing still. Ensure you have empty inventory space for any tools or materials provided at the site.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Locate the Job NPC">
    Look for the Foreman NPC typically standing near the entrance of the construction site or inside a small trailer. Interact with them to accept the task. You will usually see a prompt or a marker appearing on your HUD.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Complete Manual Tasks">
    Follow the markers to carry bricks, move planks, or interact with specific machinery. Each completed action adds a set amount of cash to your "unsecured" wallet or "dirty" cash pool depending on the current game update.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Secure Your Earnings">
    Once your wallet is full or you feel the area is getting too hot, head immediately to the Bank or the Laundromat. Do not stay at the job site with a full wallet; the risk of losing your hard-earned money is too high.
  </Step>
</Steps>

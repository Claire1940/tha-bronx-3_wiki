<Section icon={Target} title="The Wireless and Electronics Heist">

The Electronics heist, often referred to by the community as the "T-Mobile Job," is a newer addition to the criminal roster. It is a classic "smash and grab" that requires specific timing and tools. This robbery is particularly lucrative because it can be "reset" if the server is not fully occupied, allowing for multiple runs in a single night cycle.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Acquire the Sledgehammer">
    Head to the hardware store located at the bottom of the main street. Purchase a Sledgehammer for a one-time fee. This tool has infinite uses and can be stored in your backpack or vehicle trunk.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Purchase the Bank Bag">
    Visit the Marketplace (Black Market). Go to the second floor and purchase a Bank Bag. This increases your carrying capacity, allowing you to hold up to 5 phones at once.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Wait for Nightfall">
    The Wireless and Electronics shop is only robbable during the night cycle. If you attempt to smash the glass during the day, you will receive a "Not robbable at this time" notification.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Execute the Smash">
    Equip your sledgehammer and hit the display glass 4-5 times until it shatters. Walk over the phones to fill your bag. Once full, exit immediately.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Sell the Loot">
    Return to the designated buyer NPC to exchange your stolen electronics for cash. A full bag typically yields $3,250.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Pro Strategy">
If the store is still open after you sell your first bag, you can return and rob it again. There are usually enough items in the store for 3-4 full bag runs, totaling over $10,000 if you are fast enough.
</Callout>

</Section>

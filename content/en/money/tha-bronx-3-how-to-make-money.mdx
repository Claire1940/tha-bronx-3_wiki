When looting, your inventory space is your biggest bottleneck. In **tha bronx 3**, you don't just lose time when you die; you lose any cash that hasn't been banked or stored in a safe.

**Key Looting Locations:**
- **On the Radar**: Located opposite the laundromat, this is a prime spot for high-value robberies but is heavily camped by aggressive players.
- **South Bronx Housing**: Excellent for neighborhood looting runs, though the distance from the Medical Center makes it risky if you get injured.
- **Soda Pop Store**: Found in the upper Mainland Bronx, this area is essential for gathering ingredients like Sugar Bags, which can be sold or used for crafting.
